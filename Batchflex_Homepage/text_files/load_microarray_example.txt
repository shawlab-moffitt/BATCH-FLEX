<b>Load Microarray Example</b>
<p>BatchFLEX includes a sample dataset within the Shiny app, which was generated by harmonizing expression data originating from the ImmGen project.</p> 
<p>Users can access the sample dataset by simply selecting the <b>Load Example Data</b> button from the <b>"Step 1 - Data Input"</b> tab.</p>
<p>In this dataset, <b>Study</b> is considered the batch effect and <b>Major Lineage</b> or the <b>Cell Type</b> is considered the variable of interest.</p>
